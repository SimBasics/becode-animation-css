Post Malone
Austin Richard Post (born July 4, 1995),known professionally as Post Malone, is an American singer, songwriter, rapper and record producer. Known for his introspective songwriting and laconic vocal style, Malone has gained acclaim for bending a range of genres including country, grunge, hip hop and R&B. He first attained recognition in 2015 following the release of his debut single "White Iverson".
